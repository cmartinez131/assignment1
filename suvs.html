SUVs, or Sports Utility Vehicles, are a type of passenger vehicle that combines features of a passenger car and a light truck. They typically have a raised ride height, four-wheel drive, and a spacious interior that can accommodate five or more passengers. SUVs are often used for off-road activities and are favored for their versatility, spaciousness, and commanding driving position.
